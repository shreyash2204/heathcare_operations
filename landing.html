Design system

Glass where you look, flat where you act.

01

Frosted surfaces for context

Sidebar, header, cards, and modals sit on soft blurred glass so the interface always feels layered, never flat and lifeless.

02

Flat, solid controls for action

Buttons, badges, and form inputs stay flat and high-contrast, so nothing you need to click ever hides behind translucency.

03

One palette, every state

Navy for structure, teal for confirmation, orange for attention — consistent across light and dark mode.
